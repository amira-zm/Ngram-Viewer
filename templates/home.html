Sharebooks Ngram Viewer

Sharebooks Ngram Viewer is a new linguistic tool released by Sharebooks on March 3, 2022.
It allows to visualize in graphical form the evolution of the frequency of one or several words or groups of words through time in printed sources since 2003.
The application currently contains words from more than 5 million books, which according to the developers corresponds to 4% of the books ever published. The oldest books used in the project date back to the 16th century, but the vast majority are from after 1800.

It is in fact a huge searchable lexicon containing more than 500 billion words and organized in sub-lexicons by language: English (361 billion words (Mm) - differentiated lexicons for American and British English), French (45 Mm), Spanish (45 Mm), German (37 Mm), Russian (35 Mm), Chinese (13 Mm) and Hebrew (2 Mm).

These words can be in particular book/movie titles
